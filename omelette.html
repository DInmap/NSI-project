Cheese Omelette

Home Recipes Basket

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 15g of mature cheddar

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a jug and whisk well with a fork. Season with a pinch of salt.
  2. Heat the oil and butter in a medium non-stick frying pan over medium-low heat. Once the butter starts to foam, pour in the eggs and tilt to cover the base of the pan.
  3. Using a spatula, gently draw in the eggs from four points to create folds in the center. Do this once or twice, then leave the eggs to cook gently for 2-3 minutes until there's a little raw egg still in the middle.
  4. Sprinkle over the cheese and, using your spatula, gently fold the omelette in half.
  5. Switch off the heat and let the residual heat from the pan melt the cheese for 1 minute.
  6. Slide onto your plate and sprinkle over some black pepper to serve.
