Fluffy Pancakes

Home Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Serve with syrup or toppings of your choice and enjoy!
